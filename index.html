Bangkok to Alaska 2019

Around the World in 9 months

In 2019 I travelled through Europe, and then on to Bangkok. From there I decided to fulfil a dream of exploring Alaska. 8 months later I arrived in Anchorage, with over 30,00km driven and 12 flights behind me, having passed through Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, the Philippines, Australia, New Zealand, California, Oregon and Washington.
